Features

Everything you need to talk to AI

A native mobile experience packed with powerful features for interacting with your AI agents.

Real-time Streaming

WebSocket-powered conversations. See responses as they're generated with smooth, token-by-token streaming.

Multi-Provider Support

Connect to Claude, Ollama, Apple Intelligence, and OpenClaw servers. Switch between them seamlessly.

Voice Input

Native iOS speech transcription. Tap to talk and let your voice do the typing with real-time transcription.

38+ Slash Commands

Powerful command system with autocomplete. Control your AI agents with intuitive typed commands.

Biometric Security

Lock your conversations behind Face ID or Touch ID. Your data stays private and on-device.

Task Scheduling

Schedule recurring agent tasks with cron-style automation. Set it and forget it.

Deep Linking

Open any screen directly via lumiere:// URLs. Create triggers and home screen quick actions.

Favorite Messages

Save important AI responses for quick access. Build your personal knowledge base.

Apple Intelligence

Native on-device AI through Apple Foundation Models. Private, fast, and deeply integrated.

Built for power users who value privacy

Lumiere puts you in control. Connect to your own servers, keep data on-device, and customize every aspect of the experience.

  • Connect to self-hosted Ollama and OpenClaw servers
  • Biometric lock keeps your conversations secure
  • Backup and restore your server configurations
  • 8 color themes with automatic light/dark mode
  • Push notifications and background task support
